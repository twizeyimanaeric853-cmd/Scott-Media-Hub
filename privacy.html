Privacy Policy & Terms

Last updated: June 2026

1. Who We Are

Scott Media Hub is an entertainment, comedy, and commentary website based in Kigali, Rwanda, publishing videos and blog content. This policy explains how we handle information when you visit the site.

2. Information We Collect

We collect minimal information, limited to what you choose to share:

  • Contact form / Submit a Tip: name, email or phone, and any message content you provide. This is sent directly to us and is not stored on a server or database.
  • Automatic data: if advertising or analytics services are active on this site (such as Google AdSense or Google Analytics), they may collect standard technical data such as IP address, browser type, device type, and pages visited, via cookies.

3. Cookies & Advertising

This site may display ads served by third parties, such as Google AdSense. These services may use cookies to show ads based on your visits to this and other websites. You can opt out of personalized advertising through your browser settings or via Google's Ad Settings.

4. How We Use Information

Any information submitted via the Contact or Submit a Tip forms is used only to respond to you or evaluate a submission. We do not sell or share personal information with third parties beyond what's required for the site's advertising or hosting services to function.

5. Third-Party Links

This site links to external platforms including YouTube, WhatsApp, Instagram, Facebook, TikTok, and Telegram. We are not responsible for the privacy practices or content of these external sites.

6. Content & Accuracy

Scott Media Hub publishes commentary, entertainment, and reaction content. Where posts discuss events involving other individuals, content reflects publicly available information, video content, or commentary, and is presented for entertainment purposes. We aim for accuracy but do not guarantee it, and corrections can be requested via the Contact page.

7. User Submissions

Tips or story submissions sent via the Submit a Tip page are reviewed before any content is published. Submitting a tip does not guarantee it will be featured. By submitting, you confirm the information is accurate to your knowledge.

8. Children's Privacy

This site is not directed at children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect information from children.

9. Changes to This Policy

This policy may be updated periodically. Continued use of the site after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated terms.

10. Contact

For questions about this policy or to request removal of content, reach out via the Contact page.
